About Chef Bot

Chef Bot is an AI-powered chatbot designed to help you explore and create delicious recipes tailored to your preferences. Whether you are a seasoned cook or a beginner, Chef Bot can guide you through every step of the cooking process.

Features

Personalized Recipe Suggestions

Chef Bot provides multiple preparation methods and unique highlights for any dish you want to cook.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow easy-to-understand recipes customized to your chosen method and ingredients.

Seamless Line Integration

Interact with Chef Bot directly on the Line messaging platform for a smooth user experience.

How It Works

  1. Step 1: Tell Chef Bot the dish you want to make.
  2. Step 2: Receive multiple preparation methods and choose your preferred one.
  3. Step 3: Get a detailed recipe with step-by-step instructions.
  4. Step 4: Start cooking and enjoy your meal!
